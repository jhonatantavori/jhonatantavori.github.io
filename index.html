About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Columbia University, working with Prof. Gil Zussman.
My work is supported by the Fulbright Postdoctoral Fellowship, and Zuckerman STEM Leadership Postdcotroal Program.
My research interests center on networking and security, focusing on analyzing the performance of stochastic networks under attack in cloud and edge-cloud systems, microservices applications, and other distributed systems.
I completed my PhD at the Blavatnik School of Computer Science and AI, Tel Aviv Univesity, under the supervision of Prof. Hanoch Levy. My PhD thesis title was: "Behivour of Networks under Malicious Behaviour".
